I was born and raised in Minneapolis, MN and I moved to Texas six years ago. I am an identical twin and my sister and I are very close. In my free time I enjoy dancing, singing and spending time with my family and friends.

Since graduating with my bachelor's degree in Communication Studies in 2010, I have been on a journey to figure out what career path to follow. Over the last 8 years, I held many positions in various fields. I worked in different call centers doing customer service, technical support and collections. For two years I was self-employed as a Healthcare IT consultant and I traveled across the country training doctors on electronic medical records. I spent another two years working at Southwest Airlines in the internal technical support help desk.

Unfortunately, none of those positions were fullfilling to me. No matter how good the pay or benefits were, I could not see my self staying long term. I started to ask myself what I would do if pay and benefits weren't a factor. It took a lot of trial and error to discover what I actually had a passion for but in early 2018 I finally had my 'ah ha' moment. At that time, I was spending a lot of time online trying to figure out how to build my own Website. Before I knew it, I was researching coding online and I was spending several hours at a time, day after day doing it. Now I am attending a coding bootcamp and I couldn't be more excited that I am finally going to have the career I have been searching for.
